7-Day Vegan Diet Plan

Enjoy a plant-based diet that is rich in essential nutrients. Follow this 7-day plan for healthy, delicious, and vegan-friendly meals.

7-Day Vegan Diet Plan

Day 1: Cleanse & Energize

Breakfast: Green smoothie (spinach, banana, almond milk, chia seeds)

Lunch: Chickpea salad with avocado, tomato, cucumber, and olive oil dressing

Dinner: Lentil soup with carrots, celery, and spinach

Day 2: Protein Boost

Breakfast: Tofu scramble with bell peppers, spinach, and avocado

Lunch: Quinoa salad with black beans, corn, and a lime dressing

Dinner: Vegan stir-fry with tempeh, broccoli, and brown rice

Day 3: Energizing Greens

Breakfast: Overnight oats with chia seeds, almond milk, and berries

Lunch: Kale salad with quinoa, chickpeas, tahini dressing

Dinner: Zucchini noodles with pesto sauce and roasted cherry tomatoes

Day 4: Colorful Plates

Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with mixed fruits, granola, and coconut flakes

Lunch: Avocado toast with tomato, arugula, and lemon

Dinner: Vegan chili with kidney beans, black beans, and bell peppers

Day 5: Gut Health

Breakfast: Chia pudding with almond milk, flaxseeds, and strawberries

Lunch: Vegan sushi rolls with avocado, cucumber, and carrot

Dinner: Sweet potato and black bean tacos with avocado

Day 6: Vibrant & Flavorful

Breakfast: Vegan pancakes with almond butter and maple syrup

Lunch: Buddha bowl with hummus, roasted vegetables, quinoa, and avocado

Dinner: Cauliflower tacos with avocado crema and salsa

Day 7: Detox & Recharge

Breakfast: Acai bowl with granola, coconut, and berries

Lunch: Vegan lentil salad with cucumber, tomato, and olive oil dressing

Dinner: Vegan curry with coconut milk, tofu, and vegetables (carrot, peas, cauliflower)
