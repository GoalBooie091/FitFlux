Underweight Weight Gain Diet Plan

This plan is designed to help individuals who are underweight gain healthy weight.

Recommended Foods

  • High-calorie foods (avocados, nuts, seeds)
  • Protein-rich foods (chicken, fish, tofu)
  • Whole grains (brown rice, oats, quinoa)

Sample Meal Plan

Breakfast: Oats with milk and nuts

Lunch: Chicken with brown rice and vegetables

Dinner: Grilled fish with quinoa and a salad
