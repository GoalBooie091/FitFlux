Complete Weekly Routine

Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Barbell Bench Press - 4x8
  • Overhead Shoulder Press - 3x10
  • Triceps Pushdowns - 3x12

Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)

  • Deadlifts - 4x5
  • Barbell Row - 3x10
  • Hammer Curls - 3x12

Day 3: Legs

  • Back Squats - 4x8
  • Lunges - 3x10 each leg
  • Leg Press - 3x12

Day 4: Active Recovery

  • Light cardio or walking
  • Foam rolling/stretching
  • Mobility work

Day 5: Full Body Compound

  • Clean & Press - 4x6
  • Front Squats - 3x8
  • Chin-ups - 3 sets to failure
  • Farmer's Carry - 3 rounds of 30s

Day 6: Core & Stability

  • Plank - 3x60s
  • Russian Twists - 3x20
  • Leg Raises - 3x15
  • Bird-Dog - 3x10 each side

Day 7: Full Rest

  • Sleep and recover
  • Hydrate and stretch
  • Plan for the upcoming week

Tips for Success

Warm Up Properly

Always prepare your muscles and joints before lifting heavy.

Track Progress

Record weights, sets, and reps to measure improvements.

Prioritize Nutrition

Fuel your gains with a balanced, protein-rich diet.
