Strength Training Program

Build lean muscle, increase power, and develop total-body strength with science-backed workouts designed for all levels.

Weekly Strength Training Plan

Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

Barbell Bench Press

4 sets of 8 reps

Overhead and Shoulder Press

3 sets of 10 reps

Triceps Pushdowns

3 sets of 12 reps

Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)

Deadlifts

4 sets of 5 reps

Barbell Row

3 sets of 10 reps

Hammer Curls

3 sets of 12 reps

Day 3: Legs

Back Squats

4 sets of 8 reps

Lunges

3 sets of 10 reps (each leg)

Leg Press

3 sets of 12 reps

Day 4: Core

Crunches

3 sets of 20 reps

Plank

3 sets of 1 minute

Day 5: Full Body

Power Cleans

3 sets of 6 reps

Kettlebell Swings

3 sets of 15 reps

Push-Ups

3 sets of 20 reps

Day 6: Active Recovery

Engage in light movement such as:

  • Walking
  • Stretching
  • Yoga

Day 7: Rest Day

Take a full rest day to allow your muscles to recover and grow stronger.
